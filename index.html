About Us

TATVA 2022

TATVA is the annual techno-cultural festival of BNMIT organized by its students. TATVA attracts the best technical talent to compete with each other and prove their competence. TATVA brings creativity and freshness in its events by introducing at least one new event every year to bring in new challenges among the participants, the fest culminates with a Mega Event. We have a lot of events lined up, about which we will brief you shortly. Sit back and enjoy the show, we have a lot to share.
